What Are Backlinks?

Backlinks, or inbound links, are critical for SEO. They are created when one site links to another, signaling the content's quality and relevance to search engines like Google.

How Do Backlinks Work?

Search engine bots follow the links on a webpage to discover new content. If a static page links to another site, the search engine recognizes it as a backlink. The value of a backlink depends on the quality and relevance of the linking page, with high-quality backlinks from authoritative sites significantly boosting SEO.
